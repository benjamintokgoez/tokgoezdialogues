I love learning and how it can shape people's minds and our world. I also love sharing thoughts, ideas, and being challenged by others.

This blog is an experiment. Every now and then, when I walk my dog Linux, I record my thoughts and ideas — challenges I've faced at work, insights from my daily research in tech and other fields — and then use AI to challenge them deeply. The result becomes a blog post.

I truly believe that transparency is key to intellectual honesty. So every entry ships with the receipts: the finished output.md, the exact prompt.txt I used to challenge my thinking, and the sources behind it. No hidden steps.

— Benny
