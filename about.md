> Nobody ever should hide for a good walk and thinking.

I'm **Benjamin Tokgöz** (friends call me Benny). I've spent 10+ years in tech —
these days at Microsoft, working with Azure and Linux — and I study philosophy on
the side. I grew up on Linux, which is also, fittingly, the name of my dog and
my usual walking companion.

## How every entry is made

This blog runs on one simple, transparent process:

1. **The walk.** While I walk Linux, I think out loud and talk through whatever's
   on my mind — a tech announcement, a philosophical question, something from work.
2. **The challenge.** I use a conversational AI as a *critical opponent*, not a
   yes-man: its job is to push back, verify facts, and find what I've missed.
3. **The write-up.** I run that conversation through a second prompt that shapes it
   into a finished post — written entirely in my own voice.

On every post you can open the `prompt.txt` tab to read the exact prompts I used,
and the sources I leaned on. The point of the blog *is* the transparency.
